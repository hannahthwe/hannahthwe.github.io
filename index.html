Graduate Student, KAIST

With an interest in the role of digital technologies in development effectiveness, I am currently researching the adoption of AI for achieving Sustainable Development Goals and the failure of information systems in developing countries. Outside of my curricular activities, I have served as a student ambassador, and international student representative in advisory committee for KAIST international community enhancement. A passion for contributing to my community has led me to volunteer for the Women in AI - Myanmar, a non-profit organization, to promote the engagement and participation of women in tech education and industry.

Research Interests: SDGs, ICT4D, AI, System-of-Systems
